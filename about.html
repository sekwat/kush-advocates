About KAS

Kush Advocates & Solicitors (KAS) is one of emerging leading national law firm based in South Sudan. The firm specializes in a range of legal issues from litigation, legal research to corporate matters. KAS has 2 partners who are principal founders and other lawyers and associate lawyer. KAS has a global partnership with a multinational law firms in Africa, which includes, Nigeria, Sierra Leone, South Africa, Uganda and Zimbabwe.
