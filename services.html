Our Legal Services

We practice law generally and offer comprehensive services with special emphasis on the following areas: -

A. CONVEYANCING:

  • Preparation of Charges/Mortgages.
  • Preparation of Sale Agreements on Property Transactions
  • Preparation of Lease Agreements with respect to Residential and Commercial properties.
  • Preparation and Registration of transfers.
  • Preparation and Registration of Powers of Attorney, caution, caveat over property.
  • We advise, act on behalf a deal with land conveyance, lease, sub-lease, Real estate management, sale, mortgages, caveat, easement, interest in land restricted covenants etc

    • B. DEBT COLLECTION

    Both our partners have wide experience in debts collection and the subsequent arbitration and litigation.

    C. COMMERCIAL & BUSINESS LAW

  • Preparation of debentures chattels and mortgages.
  • Preparation of chartered plane agreements.
  • Preparation of bids and contracts concerning constructions
  • Preparation of imports and Exports clearance
  • Formation and Registration of companies under Companies Act.
  • Amendment to the Memorandum and Articles of Association and any other Legal work arising from the provisions of Companies Act.
  • Preparation of transfer of Business Agreements.
  • Sale of Goods and Agency relations

    • D. INSURANCE

  • Handling Insurance claims and acting for Insurance Companies,
  • Acting for insured and third party's claims

    • E. LITIGATION

  • Appearing before all classes of court in South Sudan either on Civil, Criminal matters, Constitutional matters and succession/family law matters among others.

    • F. LABOUR LAW

  • We advise on labour law disputes generally. e.g Social insurance benefits- after work, leave days, night work, child law in relation to work, women and the law in relation to work, maternity leave and paternity leave, Employer's-Employee's liabilities, Terminations of contracts (wrongful & lawful), Safety at work, vicarious liability & Employees' contracts etc.

    • G. TAX LAW

  • We act on behalf of our client in matters concerning personal income taxes, property taxes, business tax (if any) and any other tax clearance including waiving accumulated taxes and Exemption from taxes, transfer pricing and etc.

    • H. CONTRACT LAW AND PROCUREMENT ADVICE

  • We represent our client in public contracts and procurement of all kinds as well as private contracts with employees and represent our client before Donors in matters related to legal issues (when the client is an NGO)

    • I. PUBLIC INTERNATIONAL LAW

  • We advise on legal effects of treaties, dealing between/among states, immunity of International NGOs and Staff, Air and marine law, Nationality, passport & Immigration law, refugees and IDPs law, International Human rights law etc.

    • J. CONSTITUTIONAL & ADMINISTRATIVE LAW

  • We advise on interpretation of Constitutional law, Rule of law, Bills of rights, Fundamental Principles of law, administrative actions, Public policy and good governance etc.

    • K. LAW OF ARBITRATION

  • We act as arbitrators for alternative dispute resolutions, negotiations etc.

    • L. ENVIRONMENATAL & NATURAL RESOURCES LAW

  • We provide legal service on environmental protection, wildlife conservations, plants and natural environment, Air pollution, petroleum management and natural environment.

    • M. LAW OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

  • We protect the interest and rights of our clients in matters related to Trademarks, copyrights, Patents, Industrial designs, Geographic indications and passing off etc

    • N. Investment law

  • We represent foreign investors and national investors in matters related to concession agreements, tax exemption, Bilateral investment treaties (BITs), operation license, disputes settlements, Oil & gas contracts and deal making and project finance.

    • O. International Trade & Regional Trade law in Africa

  • We represent our clients to negotiate on their behalves in cross-border trade and regional agreements in Africa, imports and exports, trade facilitation and border clearance, trade finance, regional integration and common market related matters.

    • P. Human Rights

  • We represent our clients in matters concerning human rights generally. This involves South Sudanese courts, East African Court of Justice and African Court of Justice
  • We render advise to International and National NGOs on the legal matters in the Republic of South Sudan, including policies and bye-laws
  • We are currently acting for individual as well as corporate bodies in the Republic of South Sudan.

    • Q. CRIMINAL LAW

  • We represent our clients in criminal matters and proceedings generally.

    • R. FMAILY LAW AND DOMESTIC MATTERS

  • We handle issues of marriage, divorce, child custody, child adoption etc.

    • S. Pro bono Service

  • Based on Human Rights principles that, "injustice somewhere is injustice everywhere", it is our responsibility to pay back to the community by offering our legal service free where it is deservedly due.
